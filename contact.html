FAQ

Common Questions

How long does an AI project typically take? ▼

Timelines vary by scope. A focused AI chatbot or automation can be production-ready in 4–6 weeks. More complex ML systems or full products typically take 8–16 weeks. We always share a detailed project timeline after the discovery call.

Do you work with early-stage startups? ▼

Absolutely. We love working with founders at the idea stage through to scale. We offer flexible engagement models — from short advisory sprints to full-team embedding — that fit early-stage budgets and timelines.

What's your pricing model? ▼

We work on project-based fixed pricing or monthly retainers depending on the engagement type. After the discovery call, we send a detailed scoping document with transparent pricing. No hidden fees, ever.

Will you sign an NDA? ▼

Yes, always. We take client confidentiality very seriously. An NDA is standard practice for every engagement at RootsAI, signed before any technical discussions begin.

Can you work with our existing tech stack? ▼

Yes. We're stack-agnostic and integrate with your existing infrastructure. Whether you're on AWS, GCP, Azure, or a custom setup — we adapt, not the other way around.
